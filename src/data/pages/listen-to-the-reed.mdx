### 

This body of work is inspired by the literary poems written by famous Persian poets; Molana Jalal ed-din Rumi and Attar Neishabouri.

> Listen to the reed (flute), how it is complaining!  \ 
> It is complaining about the separations:  \
> I want a heart that is torn, torn from separations,  \
> so that I may explain the pain of this love. 
> 
> (Listen to the flute” by Rumi, Translation by Mostafa Momen, Princeton University, NJ, USA)
