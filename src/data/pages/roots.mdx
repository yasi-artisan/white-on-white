## The Roots

_I close my eyes Searching, to make sense of the space; Inside and out The dark but warm container of silence embraces me. I sink inside, like a snail in its shell And melt. Quietly, softly I sink inside (Pause)_

_Searching Breath in, breath out_

_A wind blows over me, Gentle, cold. I sink inside_

_Searching_

_A light shine in my shell Pleasant, bright._

_Still searching_

_I raise my head. My arms Recall memories from a place; I found my roots through my dream._

_Still, I feel suspended, fluttering between spaces inside and out!_

(A poem by Yasaman Moussavi)
