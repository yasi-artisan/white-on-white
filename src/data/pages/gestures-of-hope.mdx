Paper pulp, tree barks, and pigment.

***

The formless

The unspoken words

The unheard isolated sound

Transmitted, evolve, and revolve

Weaved to form a strong message:

“Women, life, freedom”

It crossed over the borders

walked out of the frame by breaking the borders

it turned into an action

and became a face of hope.

(Yasi, 2023)
